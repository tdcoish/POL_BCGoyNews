Nasty Woman Dr. Bonnie Henry Goes After Churches

February 12th, 2021

B.C.'s provincial health officer is seeking an injunction prohibiting gatherings by three Christian churches that are challenging her orders suspending in-person religious services. Lawyers for Dr. Bonnie Henry and B.C.'s attorney general will be in B.C. Supreme Court on Friday seeking orders against the leaders of Langley's Riverside Calvary Chapel, Abbotsford's Immanuel Covenant Reformed Church and the Free Reformed Church of Chilliwack. The province filed an application for the injunction last week along with a response to a petition by the churches and a handful of others who want to overturn Henry's orders.
